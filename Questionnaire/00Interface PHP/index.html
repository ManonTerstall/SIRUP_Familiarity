Welcome to the TV programmes recommendation study. It is part of a master project at the VU University Amsterdam in the context of the European ViSTA TV project. The aim is to gather feedback from real TV viewers on our research method to recommend pleasant and unexpected TV programmes as a way of improving the overall TV experience in the area of a growing online viewing behaviour.

This survey has 4 parts, where we will ask you the following types of questions:

  1. For 8 TV programmes indicate whether you like each of them or not.
  2. What are your TV preferences.
  3. Demographics and personalia.
  4. Based on the inputs from parts 1, 2 and 3 we generate 18 recommendations for you and we ask you to evaluate each of them.

All of your answers will be kept strictly confidential. The information provided will be used solely for the purpose of this academic research project.

Thank you for participating in the TV programmes recommendation study!
